# Product Overview

The Bureau of Wonders is a luxury brand communications and PR agency website. The site showcases the agency's services, brand story, case studies, and thought leadership content.

## Target Audience
High-end clients in luxury industries: jewelry, watches, fashion, leather goods, real estate, finance, hospitality, art, design, and insurance.

## Core Features
- Brand storytelling and company information
- Service offerings presentation
- Blog/insights/news publishing platform
- Case study showcase with structured templates
- Career opportunities listing
- Contact form for client inquiries
- CMS-driven content management for easy updates
