# Project Structure

## Site Pages
The website consists of the following main pages:

### Public Pages
- **Homepage** - Introduction to The Bureau of Wonders
- **About** - Brand story, philosophy, team, leadership, values
- **Services** - Communications & PR, Experiences & Events, CRM, Industries served
- **Blog/Insights/News** - Articles, thought leadership, news, case studies
- **Careers** - Culture statement, values, open positions
- **Contact** - Contact form and office details

## Content Types

### Case Studies
Structured template with:
- Challenge
- Strategy
- Execution
- Results
- Visual gallery

### Blog Posts
- Title and content
- Categories/tags (News, Case Study, Thought Leadership)
- Scheduling capability
- SEO fields

### Job Listings
- Position title
- Description
- Requirements

## CMS-Managed Content
- Homepage intro text
- About page sections (story, philosophy, values, team)
- Services descriptions and industries list
- Blog posts and case studies
- Career listings
- Contact information (address, phone, email)
- SEO metadata (page titles, meta descriptions, OG data)
