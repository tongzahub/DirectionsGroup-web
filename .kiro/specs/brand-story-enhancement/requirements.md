# Requirements Document

## Introduction

This specification defines the requirements for enhancing The Bureau of Wonders' Brand Story page using StoryBrand framework principles. The enhancement will transform the current about page into a compelling narrative that positions clients as heroes and The Bureau as their guide, following proven storytelling methodologies that drive engagement and conversions.

## Glossary

- **StoryBrand Framework**: A marketing framework that uses story structure to clarify brand messaging
- **Hero's Journey**: Narrative structure where the customer is the hero, not the brand
- **Brand Story System**: The integrated CMS and frontend components that manage brand narrative content
- **Conversion Elements**: UI components designed to guide users toward desired actions
- **Narrative Sections**: Distinct content blocks that tell parts of the brand story

## Requirements

### Requirement 1

**User Story:** As a luxury brand decision-maker, I want to quickly understand how The Bureau of Wonders can solve my communication challenges, so that I can determine if they're the right partner for my brand.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a user visits the brand story page, THE Brand Story System SHALL display a clear problem statement that resonates with luxury brand challenges within the first viewport
2. WHEN a user scrolls through the narrative, THE Brand Story System SHALL present The Bureau as the experienced guide with proven expertise in luxury communications
3. THE Brand Story System SHALL include specific success metrics and client outcomes to demonstrate authority
4. WHERE social proof is displayed, THE Brand Story System SHALL showcase recognizable luxury brand testimonials and case study highlights
5. WHEN a user reaches the end of the story, THE Brand Story System SHALL provide clear next steps with prominent call-to-action elements

### Requirement 2

**User Story:** As a content manager, I want to easily update brand story elements through the CMS, so that I can maintain fresh and relevant messaging without technical assistance.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Brand Story System SHALL provide editable fields for each story section including hero statement, problem definition, solution positioning, and success stories
2. WHEN content is updated in the CMS, THE Brand Story System SHALL reflect changes on the frontend within 60 seconds
3. THE Brand Story System SHALL support rich text formatting for narrative content including emphasis, lists, and embedded media
4. WHERE testimonials are managed, THE Brand Story System SHALL allow addition, editing, and reordering of client quotes with attribution
5. THE Brand Story System SHALL include preview functionality to review changes before publishing

### Requirement 3

**User Story:** As a website visitor on mobile, I want the brand story to be engaging and easy to navigate, so that I can understand The Bureau's value proposition regardless of my device.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN accessed on mobile devices, THE Brand Story System SHALL display content in optimized single-column layouts with appropriate typography scaling
2. THE Brand Story System SHALL implement progressive disclosure techniques to prevent cognitive overload on smaller screens
3. WHEN users interact with story elements, THE Brand Story System SHALL provide smooth animations and transitions that enhance rather than distract from the narrative
4. THE Brand Story System SHALL maintain loading performance under 3 seconds on mobile networks
5. WHERE interactive elements exist, THE Brand Story System SHALL ensure touch targets meet accessibility guidelines with minimum 44px dimensions

### Requirement 4

**User Story:** As a marketing director, I want the brand story to guide visitors toward conversion actions, so that we can generate qualified leads from our storytelling efforts.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Brand Story System SHALL strategically place conversion elements at natural story transition points without disrupting narrative flow
2. WHEN users engage with the story content, THE Brand Story System SHALL track interaction metrics including scroll depth, time on section, and conversion rates
3. THE Brand Story System SHALL implement A/B testing capabilities for different story variations and call-to-action placements
4. WHERE contact forms are embedded, THE Brand Story System SHALL pre-populate relevant context based on the story section where engagement occurred
5. THE Brand Story System SHALL integrate with analytics platforms to measure story effectiveness and conversion attribution

### Requirement 5

**User Story:** As a brand strategist, I want the story structure to follow proven frameworks, so that our messaging resonates with target audiences and drives business results.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Brand Story System SHALL implement the seven-part StoryBrand framework structure including character, problem, guide, plan, call-to-action, success, and failure scenarios
2. WHEN presenting The Bureau's expertise, THE Brand Story System SHALL position the agency as the empathetic guide rather than the hero of the story
3. THE Brand Story System SHALL clearly articulate the stakes involved in luxury brand communication decisions
4. WHERE success stories are presented, THE Brand Story System SHALL focus on client transformations and measurable outcomes
5. THE Brand Story System SHALL maintain consistent messaging hierarchy that supports the overarching narrative throughout all story sections
